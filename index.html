UEFA EURO 2024

European Championship

UEFA EURO 2024, hosted by Germany from June 14 to July 14, will unite 24 European teams in a thrilling competition. Matches will take place in 10 cities, including Berlin and Munich, blending historic and modern atmospheres. This year’s tournament focuses on sustainability, inclusivity, and world-class football excitement.

Locations

10 Cities - 10 stadiums

UEFA EURO 2024 will take place in 10 iconic German cities, including Berlin, Munich, Hamburg, and Frankfurt. Each location offers a unique experience, from Berlin’s rich history to Munich’s vibrant culture and Hamburg’s scenic harbor. These cities will showcase Germany’s hospitality and passion for football.
